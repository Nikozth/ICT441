Basic General Mathematics Course:

  • Welcome to Basic General Mathematics, a course designed for those who wish to build a foundation in mathematics. Whether you are a beginner in mathematics or someone looking to consolidate the basics, this course will provide you with a comprehensive and practical mathematics learning experience.
  • Basic mathematical operations: Learn addition, subtraction, multiplication and division and other basic mathematical operations to establish basic mathematical skills. Algebra and equations: master the basic concepts of algebra, learn to solve equations and inequalities. Fundamentals of geometry: Understand the basic principles of geometry, including the properties of figures, the calculation of area and volume.
  • Fractions and percentages: Learn the concepts of fractions and percentages and master their application in real life. Data and statistics: Understand basic data collection and analysis methods and learn to make simple statistical charts. Application of practical problems: Apply the mathematical knowledge to practical problems, and cultivate the ability to solve mathematical problems in real life.
  • Practical orientation: With practical problems as the background, students can apply mathematical knowledge to real life. Personalized tutoring: Provide personalized math tutoring and support based on the student's learning progress and needs. Interactive learning: Enhance students' math learning experience through group discussions and practical exercises.
  • Upon completion of this course, you will have: Solid mathematical foundation, able to use basic mathematical knowledge to solve daily problems. Improve mathematical thinking and problem solving skills. More confident in facing math-related learning and work challenges.
