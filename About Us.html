About Ningbo University Learning Center

  • Welcome to the Ningbo University Learning Center, where we are committed to providing a full range of academic support and learning resources to create a positive and dynamic learning environment for our students. The Learning Center is an academic community that brings together knowledge, develops skills, and fosters collaboration to help students succeed and achieve personal growth.

    • Founded with a vision to empower minds and foster academic excellence, the Learning Center at Ningbo University has a rich history dating back to its establishment. Over the years, it has evolved into a dynamic hub of knowledge, innovation, and community engagement. Since its inception, the Learning Center has played a pivotal role in shaping the educational landscape of Ningbo University.

  • Core services of the Learning Center:

    • Library resources: The Learning Center has a wealth of library resources covering various subject areas, providing students with in-depth academic materials and a rich reading experience.
    Academic Guidance: We provide professional academic guidance services, including course guidance, writing guidance and subject knowledge training, to help students better understand and master the content of the study.
    Modern learning facilities: The learning Center is equipped with advanced learning facilities, including computer LABS, independent study areas and multimedia classrooms, providing students with a variety of learning Spaces.

  • The concept of the Learning Center:

    • Academic Excellence: We strive for academic excellence and encourage our students to pursue knowledge, explore academic fields, and become future leaders with innovative thinking.

    Cooperation and win-win: The learning center emphasizes the concept of cooperation and win-win, and cultivates students' teamwork and communication skills through teamwork and mutual learning.

    Personality Development: We are committed to supporting each student's personal development, providing personalized academic guidance and career planning advice to help them achieve their dreams and goals.

  • Join us to explore the mystery of knowledge and achieve both academic and personal harvest!
