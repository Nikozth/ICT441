Basic English Course:

  • Welcome to the Basic English Course, a course designed for beginners to help you build a solid foundation in English and improve your language communication skills. Whether you are a beginner or a student looking to consolidate your foundation, this course will provide you with a systematic and practical learning experience.
  • Language basics: Learning the basic grammatical structure, vocabulary and common expressions of English. Listening and speaking: Improve listening comprehension ability and cultivate daily speaking ability. Reading comprehension: Reading simple articles to improve reading comprehension and vocabulary skills.
  • Writing Basics: Learn basic writing structure, including sentence formation and paragraph organization. Daily dialogue: Simulate the dialogue of common scenes in daily life to improve practical communication skills. Cultural exchange: understand the cultural differences of English-speaking countries and promote cross-cultural communication.
  • nteractive learning: Through role play, group discussion, etc., to enhance students' sense of participation. Practical application: Apply the knowledge to practical scenarios to improve the language ability. Personalized learning: Provide personalized learning support according to the level and needs of students.
  • By the end of the Basic English Course, you will have gained confidence in using English in everyday situations. You will be able to communicate effectively, understand written texts, and express your thoughts and ideas accurately.
  • So, whether you want to improve your English for personal or professional reasons, the Basic English Course is a great choice to start your language learning journey.
